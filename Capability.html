Curriculum workshop

The content developed will be aligned with the IT industry. The content progression will be gradual and designed to cover foundation, core, and advanced subjects. The courseware is developed by a team of industry veterans and Subject matter experts to ensure content relevancy.

Capability development program for faculty

The capability development program for faculty will be delivered before the commencement of the semester – usually at the skill park/university. The content will be available on the Knowbots portal. In addition, an assessment will be conducted for the faculty to evaluate their understanding of the course—rich experiential training on various tools for the courses offered.

Industry guest lectures

SMEs (Subject Matter Experts) on each technology/domain will visit the skill park/spokes and deliver guest lectures to students. In addition, Webinars are conducted regularly to connect educators with the educated. These guest lectures are part of the specialization courses embedded in the curriculum. The SMEs are also available for interaction with the students as a mentor through the platform while doing the projects.

Content delivery

The content will be delivered in a hybrid model. The rich content and experiential learning guide will be provided for all the students. In addition, virtual classes and virtual labs capability is offered to enhance the quality of learning and are available remotely to access.
