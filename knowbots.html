/ Knowbots

Knowbots is a solution with enhancements, customizations and configurations to deliver a powerful, scalable and secure system, which will work as the web-based learning management system for the client.

Knowbots is the backbone of the comprehensive VLE (Virtual Learning Environment) and can be used:

  • As the central content repository and gateway to the digital learning assets
  • To manage and provide access to E-Learning modules.
  • To create and assign interactive assessments.
  • To upload audio & video files, documents such as PPTs and PDFs for the employees/ students.
